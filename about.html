What we do

Regal American Homes has earned its reputation as one of Arizona’s premier luxury home builders. Before our beginnings in 2014, we saw a void in the luxury home market. While many builders were creating beautiful homes, they failed to take the uniqueness of each individual home into consideration. Rather than fitting the mold and creating standard homes, we set out create quality, luxury homes with character...homes that are as special as our clients are. In 4 years, we have created over X custom luxury homes for families of all shapes and sizes. We know that a house is so much more than the walls you live within; it’s a home where you can make memories for years to come. As a Phoenix-based company, we take pride in creating one-of-a-kind homes that can be seen across the valley. Our mission here at Regal is simple: we want to come alongside you and make your dreams of owning a home come true. Whether you’re looking to buy a new home, build a custom home, or trade your current home, we can work with you!
